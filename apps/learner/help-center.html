Getting Started

Everything you need to know to begin your NMCN exam preparation journey.

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Click on "Sign Up" on our homepage
  2. Fill in your personal information (name, email, phone number)
  3. Create a secure password
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile information

Once registered, you can access free study materials and choose a subscription plan for premium features.

We offer several subscription plans to fit your needs:

  • Free Plan: Access to basic study materials and limited mock exams
  • Monthly Plan: Full access to all features including unlimited mock exams and daily tutorials
  • Quarterly Plan: Same benefits as monthly at a discounted rate
  • Annual Plan: Best value with maximum savings and additional premium features

All paid plans include access to our 20,000+ question bank and personalized progress tracking.

To start practicing with mock exams:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Navigate to the "Mock Exams" section
  3. Choose your preferred exam mode (timed or practice)
  4. Select the number of questions and subject areas
  5. Click "Start Exam" to begin
  6. Review your results and explanations after completion

We recommend starting with practice mode to familiarize yourself with the question format before attempting timed exams.

Account Management

Manage your profile, settings, and account preferences.

To reset your password:

  1. Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Use your new password to log in

Password reset links are valid for 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.

To update your profile information:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "My Profile" or "Account Settings"
  3. Edit the information you want to update
  4. Click "Save Changes" to update your profile

You can update your name, phone number, nursing school, and other personal details at any time.

Payment & Billing

Everything about payments, subscriptions, and billing questions.

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Paystack (including card payments, bank transfers, and USSD)
  • Debit and Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Bank Transfer
  • Mobile Money

All payments are processed securely through Paystack, Nigeria's leading payment processor.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time:

  1. Go to "Account Settings"
  2. Click on "Subscription Management"
  3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Follow the confirmation steps

Please note that as per our terms of service, we do not offer refunds for canceled subscriptions. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

If your payment fails:

  1. Check that your card details are correct
  2. Ensure you have sufficient funds
  3. Try a different payment method
  4. Contact your bank if the issue persists
  5. Reach out to our support team if problems continue

Sometimes payments fail due to network issues or bank security measures. Trying again after a few minutes often resolves the issue.

Technical Support

Resolving technical issues and platform problems.

If the platform is loading slowly:

  1. Check your internet connection speed
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
  5. Restart your device
  6. Try accessing the platform at a different time

If issues persist, contact our technical support team with details about your device and browser.

If you can't see your progress or scores:

  1. Ensure you're logged into the correct account
  2. Refresh the page or restart the app
  3. Check that you've completed exams (practice mode may not save scores)
  4. Verify your subscription includes progress tracking
  5. Contact support if the issue continues

Progress tracking is available for paid subscribers. Free plan users have limited access to this feature.
