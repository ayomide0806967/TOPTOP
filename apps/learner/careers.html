Academic Nightingale unites product minds, nurse educators, data scientists, and storytellers. Together we design joyful prep experiences that transform high-stakes exams into daily wins for caregivers across Nigeria and beyond.

Designing nurse-ready technology for intelligent hospitals

We are building the continent’s first prep platform that pairs NMCN mastery with fluency in AI-assisted care. From decision support to documentation automation, every product sprint helps nurses navigate the smart wards already emerging across Africa.

Our upcoming curriculum blends clinical reasoning with data storytelling, healthcare informatics, telehealth etiquette, and safety-first automation guidelines. If you love translating complex technology into bedside-ready skills, you’ll feel at home here.
